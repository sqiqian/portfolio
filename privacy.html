Privacy & Data Ethics

As a researcher in Child Psychology and a digital creator, I believe that privacy is a fundamental human right. This page outlines my commitment to protecting your digital footprint and my own intellectual property.

"In this space, your data is not a product, and my creative work is not a dataset."

1. Zero-Tracking

I do not use cookies, trackers, or third-party analytics (like Google Analytics) on this website. You are free to explore this portfolio without being followed.

2. Intellectual Property & Anti-AI Scraping

All content on this site—including my psychology assignments, interactive fiction, and artwork—is my original intellectual property. I do not consent to this content being used for:

  • Training Generative AI models.
  • Automated data scraping or "crawling" for commercial use.
  • Redistribution without explicit written permission.

3. Personal Data

The only personal data I receive is information you voluntarily provide via email. This information is used strictly for professional communication and will never be shared, sold, or added to a mailing list without your consent.

4. External Links

My portfolio contains links to external sites (like my Twine games or London Study Spots). Please note that I am not responsible for the privacy practices of those external platforms.
